My Profile

As a college student, I wear many hats.

Passionate Student

I'm a 3rd year UC Berkeley student studying Computer Science and Cognitive Science. Fun fact: other students like me commonly refer to our unique (and awesome) double major as CS2! To find out more details of my studies, including classes I've taken, classes I'm currently taking, and much more please see the Education section.

Throughout first couple years at Berkeley, I immersed myself in my studies and extra curriculars in order to truly find what interested me. Cognitive science has since been a passion of mine; I find the human mind absolutely fascinating.

Lucky for me, my cognitive studies led me to yet another enthralling area of study: Computer Science. My love for CS has definitely evolved over the years, but I can proudly say that it now occupies the majority of my interest. I've heard this said before- and I agree- that computer engineering is the closest thing to magic I can think of.

Manager

I'm the proud manager of the UC Men's Chorale- an amazing singing group comprised of 20 talented men. Every semester we put on and participate numerous events. We sing a wide range of music from fight songs at graduations to Beethoven's 9th in the Berkeley Greek Theater, from medieval Germanic Carmina Burana to weekly performances on Sproul Plaza.

In addition to UCMC Manager, I am also the Marketing Chair for the larger UC Chorales and Ensembles. UCCE is an exceptional musical organization comprised of several a capella groups and choirs, including the Men's Chorale. Holding the positions of Marketing Chair and UCMC Manager is a lot of work, but it's totally worth bringing school spirit and music across campus.

What I do For Fun

When I'm not working or studying, I fill my time with a wide range of activities.

Swimming

I've always loved the water. In high school I was Swim Team and Water Polo team captain, and I've been lifeguarding ever since I could have a job. I'm always ready to go swimming- in a pool or at the beach.

Singing

I've been singing ever since I can remember- well, in the shower at least. But in the spring of 2015 I decided to join the UC Men's Chorale. Since then the people I've met and the experiences I've had turned singing into a part of my life. There's something about being my own musical instrument that just can't be beat.

Rock Climbing

Ok so maybe I'm not a pro- but ever since I've been exposed to rock climbing and slack lining it always makes me feel accomplished. Whenever I have the time, I grab a friend and hit the nearest bouldering gym. Tons of fun and an amazing workout- it's never a mistake.

Eating

Yes I consider eating an interest of mine. I love food! I mean who doesn't? From escargot in Paris to a gallon of ice cream back home, there are few things I enjoy more than good food with good company.

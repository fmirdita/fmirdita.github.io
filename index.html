What I do For Fun

When I'm not working or studying, I fill my time with a wide range of activities.

Swimming

I've always loved the water. In high school I was Swim Team and Water Polo team captain, and I've been lifeguarding ever since I could have a job. I'm always ready to go swimming- in a pool or at the beach.

Singing

I've been singing ever since I can remember- well, in the shower at least. But in the spring of 2015 I decided to join the UC Men's Chorale. Since then the people I've met and the experiences I've had turned singing into a part of my life. There's something about being my own musical instrument that just can't be beat.

Learning a new Language

I think languages are fascinating. I'm always learning a new langugage. I studied French for about 6 years, in and out of the class room. After finally visiting France, I decided to embark on a journey with German. I've got plenty of languages on my list, and plan on learning all of them.

Developping

The internet is amazing. And coding is amazing. And together, I can't think of anything that comes closer to magic. So in my spare time I love to learn something about web development- every time I do I become that much closer to being an actual wizard.

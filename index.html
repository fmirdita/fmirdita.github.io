Projects

Autism Buddie

Android application with wear component, designed to help autistic children and their parents. The phone application is used by the adult, while the child wears the watch. The watch is specially themed to whatever the child likes, engaging the kid with trivia and social skills. The parent can choose to set the watch to different modes, send reminders to the kid, and change the theme of the watch from the phone.

Pollitik

This phone apllication features an Android wear component as well. With this app, you can look up your US Representatives based on your location or a zipcode. You can view a bunch of details, browse through the reps on your watch, and view 2012 presidential election results for a given district. This project was completed for a Human-Computer Interfaces and Design course, CS160.
